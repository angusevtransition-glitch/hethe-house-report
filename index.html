Why the solar payback is faster than the battery: The solar costs less per pound of annual return (£5,956 for £926/yr vs £7,499 for £1,029/yr). Its value comes from two sources: reducing your exposure to the 33.72p peak rate by 1,546 kWh (£521/yr saved), and earning £217/yr from exporting surplus electricity to the grid. On sunny days the panels generate more than the house and battery can absorb, so the excess is sold back at 12p/kWh.
